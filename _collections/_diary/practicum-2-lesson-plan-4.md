<h4>1. General Objectives</h4>
<p>Students will be able to:</p>
<ul>
  <li>develop mathematical curiosity when solving problems.</li>
  <li>become confident in using mathematics to analyse and solve problems both in school and in real-life situations.</li>
  <li>develop the knowledge, skills, and attitudes necessary to pursue further studies in mathematics.</li>
</ul>

<h4>2. Specific Objective (SLOs)</h4>
<p>Students will be able to:</p>
<ul>
  <li>recall what a triangle is?</li>
  <li>differentiate between types by sides scalene, isosceles, and equilateral.</li>
  <li>locate some examples of triangles from daily.</li>
</ul>

<h4>3. Instructional Aids</h4>
<p>Blackboard, chalks, textbook, triangles made of charts and scale.</p>

<h4>4. Motivational Activity</h4>
<p>To grab the attention of the students the teacher would ask a student to come on the board. Then the teacher will draw three random lines on the board and ask the student to draw a shape of his/her liking. If the student draws the triangle shape, then the teacher will ask the following questions.</p>
<ul>
  <li>How many sides are in this shape?</li>
  <li>How many angles are in this shape?</li>
  <li>What is this shape called?</li>
</ul>
<p>Announcement... today we are going to learn about triangles.</p>

<h4>5. Supervised Study</h4>
<p>Before class, prepare some flash cards with different triangles on them. For example, scalene, isosceles, equilateral triangles.</p>
<p>The teacher will distribute the triangles in the class asking students to measure the length of the sides of the triangle.</p>

<h4>6. Lecture</h4>
<p>The teacher will explain the definitions and properties of each type: scalene (all sides different), isosceles (two equal sides), equilateral (all sides equal).</p>

<h4>7. Assessment</h4>
<p>The teacher would summarize the whole class by asking different questions from the students. Such as</p>
<ul>
  <li>Tell me what a triangle is?</li>
  <li>What is the name of the triangle which has three different sides?</li>
  <li>What is the name of the triangle which has two equal sides?</li>
  <li>What is the name of the triangle which has three equal sides?</li>
</ul>

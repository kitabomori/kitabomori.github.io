      <ul>

      </ul>
    </td>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Give each student a copy of the short poem "The Blind Boy" and ask them to read it silently to themselves.</li>
        <li>Then, have them pair up with a partner to discuss the following questions:</li>
        <ol>
          <li>What is the poem about?</li>
          <li>What are the main ideas or themes in the poem?</li>
          <li>What is the message the poet is trying to convey?</li>
        </ol>
        <li>Encourage students to write and share their writeups with the class.</li>

<h4>2. Required Material</h4>
<p>Board (white/black), chalk/marker, duster</p>

<h4>3. Brainstorming</h4>
<p>The teacher will draw a tree diagram on the board and ask students to recall the point, line, ray, line segment, parallel, perpendicular, intersecting lines, and angle and its types.</p>

<h4>4. Lecture</h4>
<p>After knowing the prior knowledge of the students, the teacher will come to the main topic, i.e., "adjacent, complementary and supplementary angles", and explain the concept.</p>

<h4>5. Discussion and Drill</h4>
<p>The teacher will give some problems related to "adjacent, complementary and supplementary angles" and give time to practice them. Meanwhile, the teacher will check the students' work and answer their queries.</p>

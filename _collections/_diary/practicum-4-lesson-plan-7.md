<h4>4. KWL Plan</h4>
<table style="border-collapse:collapse; margin:1rem 0; font-size:0.95rem; width:100%; border:1px solid #333;">
  <tr>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Know</th>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Want to Know</th>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Learned</th>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>What types of reading do you enjoy?</li>
        <li>How do you usually read? (silently, out loud, in groups)</li>
        <li>Have you ever heard of or tried loud reading before?</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>What questions do you have about read out loud?</li>
        <li>Why might people choose to read out loud?</li>
        <li>Are there specific benefits you're curious about?</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Reading out loud can enhance comprehension by providing a different sensory input.</li>
        <li>Loud reading can improve fluency and oral communication skills.</li>
        <li>Expressive reading with proper tone and pronunciation adds depth to the understanding of the text.</li>
        <li>Students may discover personal preferences for reading styles, recognizing the benefits of loud reading.</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>

<h4>2. Required Material</h4>
<p>Board (white/black), chalk/marker, duster</p>

<h4>3. Demonstration</h4>
<p>The teacher will demonstrate how to greet and start a conversation by introducing himself and sharing his personal interests and experience with the class.</p>

<h4>4. Group Discussion</h4>
<p>The teacher will divide the students into pairs/groups and instruct them to jot down a few personal interests. Next, the students will discuss among themself, and the teacher will help when students face any difficulties.</p>

<h4>5. Presentation</h4>
<p>After discussions, the students will come to the front of the class and tell their classmates about each other.</p>

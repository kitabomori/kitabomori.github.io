<h4>1. Learning Outcomes</h4>

<p><strong>Students will be able to:</strong></p><ul><li><strong>I.</strong> demonstrate oral communication <em>(30%)</em></li><li><strong>II.</strong> engage in more prolonged greetings <em>(35%)</em></li><li><strong>III.</strong> develop a conversation about personal interests <em>(35%)</em></li></ul>

<p style="font-size:0.8rem; color:#888;">(R = remembering, U = understanding, A = applying, An = analysing, E = evaluating, C = creating)</p>

<h4>2. Required Material</h4>

<p>Board (white/black), chalk/marker, duster.</p>

<h4>3. Demonstration</h4>

<p>The teacher will demonstrate how to greet and start a conversation by introducing himself and sharing his personal interests and experience with the class.</p>

<h4>4. Group Discussion</h4>

<p>The teacher will divide the students into pairs/groups and instruct them to jot down a few personal interests. Next, the students will discuss among themselves, and the teacher will help when students face any difficulties.</p>

<h4>5. Presentation</h4>

<p>After discussions, the students will come to the front of the class and tell their classmates about each other.</p>

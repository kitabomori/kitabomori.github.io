<h4>1. Learning Outcomes</h4>

<p><strong>Students will be able to:</strong></p><ul><li><strong>I.</strong> recall point, line, ray, and line segment <em>(30%)</em></li><li><strong>II.</strong> recognise types of angles such as acute, right, obtuse, and straight angles <em>(35%)</em></li><li><strong>III.</strong> solve problems related to supplementary and complementary angles <em>(35%)</em></li></ul>

<p style="font-size:0.8rem; color:#888;">(R = remembering, U = understanding, A = applying, An = analysing, E = evaluating, C = creating)</p>

<h4>2. Required Material</h4>

<p>Board (white/black), chalk/marker, duster.</p>

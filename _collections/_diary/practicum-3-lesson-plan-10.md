<h4>2. Required Material</h4>
<p>Board (white/black), chalk/marker, duster</p>

<table style="border-collapse:collapse; margin:1rem 0; font-size:0.95rem; width:100%; border:1px solid #333;">
  <tr>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Engagement</th>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Start the class by asking students to share their favourite memories about school.</li>
        <li>Then, write a few sentences on the board to set an example for the students.</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Exploration</th>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Divide students into pairs and give them a few sentences to analyse.</li>
        <li>The sentences should contain simple, compound, and complex sentences.</li>
        <li>Students should identify the subject and verb.</li>
        <li>Ask each group to share their findings with the rest of the class.</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Explanation</th>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Write the definitions of sentence, subject and verb on the board.</li>
        <li>Explain the differences between the subject and the verb by examples.</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Elaboration</th>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Give students a few words to construct sentences.</li>
        <li>Allow students to work in pairs or groups to complete the exercises.</li>
        <li>Ask each group to share their sentences with the class, highlighting the subject and verb.</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Evaluation</th>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Give students a short quiz to assess their understanding of sentence construction, subject, verb, and punctuation use.</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>

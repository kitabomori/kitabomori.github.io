<h4>4. 5E Plan</h4>
<table style="border-collapse:collapse; margin:1rem 0; font-size:0.95rem; width:100%; border:1px solid #333;">
  <tr>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Engagement</th>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Start the course with a quick review of the previous class on free writing.</li>
        <li>Ask students to share their thoughts on the concept of free writing based on the previous lesson</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Exploration</th>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Remind students of the definition of freelance writing and its benefits.</li>
        <li>Introduce additional incentives for free writing and encourage students to explore different texts or topics. Example: Describe a moment that changed your mind or write a conversation between two people with opposing viewpoints.</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Explanation</th>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Facilitate class discussion about the value of sharing and receiving feedback</li>
        <li>Incorporate the idea of revision and discipline into your writing.</li>
        <li>Discuss specific areas of writing that could be improved, such as word choice, sentence structure, and clarity of explanation.</li>
        <li>Based on the comments, model the process of modifying the text.</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Elaboration</th>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Have students review an earlier text for free or use one of the new prompts.</li>
        <li>In pairs or small groups, students edit their writing and provide constructive feedback.</li>
        <li>Encourage students to focus on specific aspects of the text discussed in class, such as adding emotional detail, using sentence structure, etc.</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; background:#f5f5f5; text-align:center; font-weight:600;">Evaluation</th>
    <td style="padding:6px 12px; border:1px solid #333; vertical-align:top;">
      <ul>
        <li>Ask each student to reflect on the information they have received and the process of making changes to their writing. Facilitate class discussion of her challenges and successes.</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>

Call for Submissions: Voices on Reading in Pakistan

EduWings invites researchers, teachers, students, and policymakers to contribute blogs, op-eds, letters, and book reviews.

EduWings is currently accepting submissions on themes of education, literacy, and reading — in Pakistan and around the world.

We particularly welcome voices from:

  • Teachers working in Pakistan’s government schools
  • Researchers studying literacy and reading
  • Students who want to write about their own educational experiences
  • Policymakers engaged with curriculum or teacher education

We accept blogs, op-eds, letters, book reviews, and reflections. Please read our Guidelines before submitting, or contact us with any questions.
